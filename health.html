Health and Wellness Resources

Welcome to our dedicated health and wellness page, where you can find resources and programs aimed at supporting your physical and mental health.

Physical Health

Explore resources for nutrition, exercise, and health services available on campus.

  • Campus Gym Facilities: Located across the campus with access to students for free.
  • Nutritional Advice and Planning: Free consultations available with our campus dieticians.
  • Health Clinic Services: On-campus clinics providing check-ups, vaccinations, and medical consultations.

Mental Health

Access programs and support for mental health, including counseling and stress management workshops.

  • Counseling Center: Offering free psychological counseling and therapy sessions.
  • Stress Management Workshops: Scheduled throughout the year to help you manage life at college.
  • Peer Support Groups: Join groups to discuss and share your experiences with fellow students.

Wellness Programs

Participate in wellness programs designed to foster a healthy lifestyle and positive campus environment.

  • Yoga and Meditation Classes: Regular classes to promote mental and physical well-being.
  • Wellness Challenges: Participate in campus-wide challenges to improve health and compete for prizes.
  • Health Fairs: Annual events featuring health screenings, exhibits, and guest speakers.
